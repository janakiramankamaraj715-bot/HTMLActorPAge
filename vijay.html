Vijay

Ajith Kumar is a prominent Indian actor primarily known for his work in Tamil cinema, recognized for his versatility across genres like romance, drama, and action. Also known as "Thala" (leader), he is a former motorcar racer who competed in the British Formula 3 season and is also known for his humility, interest in photography, cooking, and being a voracious reader. He is a private person, avoids the limelight, and has even dissolved his fan clubs to encourage fans to focus on their own lives.
